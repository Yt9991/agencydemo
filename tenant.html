About eSign & Digital Signature Integration

Digital signing can be integrated via trusted providers like DocuSign, Adobe Sign, or HelloSign.

Workflow overview:

  • Send signing link to clients (Party A, Party B) and involved parties.
  • Upon completion, each signed copy is authenticated with timestamps and audit trails.
  • Signed copies are automatically distributed to company, salesperson, and clients.

You can implement webhook listeners or API polling to track signing status and trigger notifications or document storage.
